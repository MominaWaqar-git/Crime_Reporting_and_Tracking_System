 
Crime Reporting and Tracking System 
1 Introduction 
Crime Vision is a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens, police 
officers, and administrators. It aims to replace traditional manual reporting methods with 
a centralized, efficient, and digital crime management system. The platform improves 
communication, transparency, and case management while ensuring better public safety. 
2 Problem Statement 
The current manual crime reporting system faces several challenges, including: 
• Difficulties in record-keeping and excessive paperwork. 
• Challenges in tracking the progress of reported cases. 
• Lack of effective communication between citizens and law enforcement authorities. 
• Limited public awareness regarding crime incidents and safety alerts. 
These issues result in inefficiency, delays, and reduced transparency in crime manage
ment processes. 
3 Key Objectives and Solutions 
Crime Vision provides a modern solution through the following features: 
• Online Crime Reporting: Citizens can submit crime reports directly through 
the platform. 
• Centralized Management: Administrators can review reports, approve them, 
and assign officers to investigate cases. 
• Real-Time Communication: Secure chat functionality and broadcast alerts fa
cilitate communication between citizens and authorities. 
• Data Tracking: Officers can manage investigations, update case statuses, and 
maintain criminal records efficiently. 

4 System Roles 
4.1 Administrator 
The Administrator is responsible for: 
• Managing users. 
• Reviewing and approving crime reports. 
• Assigning officers to cases. 
• Sending alerts and notifications. 
4.2 Officer 
The Officer is responsible for: 
• Investigating assigned cases. 
• Updating case progress and status. 
• Maintaining criminal records. 
4.3 Citizen 
The Citizen can: 
• Register and log into the system. 
• Report crimes online. 
• Track case status. 
• Communicate with authorities through the platform. 
5 Future Enhancements 
To further improve public safety and system efficiency, the following features are planned: 
• AI-based crime prediction and analysis. 
• Mobile application development. 
• Emergency SOS and GPS tracking. 
• Video evidence analysis using advanced technologies. 

6 Conclusion 
Crime Vision enhances transparency, efficiency, and public safety by digitizing law en
forcement workflows. The system serves as a modern bridge between citizens and police 
authorities, enabling better communication, faster response times, and improved case 
management.
